Subtitle to STL EBU Helpbook

Subtitles to STL EBU lets you convert your exl, srt, txt and rtf subtitles to STL EBU and vice versa.
This version supports the Latin Alphabet only.
STL EBU is the worldwide standard for professional subtitling distribution.

This guide is divided into four chapters:

1.- A Quick Look At The Interface and general considerations.

2.- Convert To STL EBU Step-By-Step Guide.

3.- Convert From STL EBU Step-By-Step Guide.

4.- Extra information.
