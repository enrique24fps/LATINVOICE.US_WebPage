4.- Extra Information

4.1.- Check, Uncheck, Reset Metadata Options.

This area has four buttons:

The “Check / uncheck raised & italic options” button will toggle the options “Keep raiseds sign in the text field” and “Italics in the text field only” in area 4, as well as “Consolidate double raised” in area 5.

The “Check / uncheck extra files” button will toggle option “Save report in a rtf file” in area 4, and “Save metadata in a rtf file” in area 5.

The “Reset metadata text field” will reset the metadata in area 1.

The “Exit” button quits the application.

4.2.- Text-Based Subtitle Formats examples

STL EBU, STL EBU V2 and EXL formats are data or binary formats. It’s impractical to show an example of these formats since they are a series of characters that only dedicated software is able to decode.

con cuatro millones en efectivo.
Nadie la vigila.

4

BIENVENIDOS A L.A.

5
00:00:22.451 –> 00:00:25.220

Tengo que abrir el club.
